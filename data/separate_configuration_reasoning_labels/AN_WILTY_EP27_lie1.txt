Nodding might signal agreement, attentiveness, and genuine engagement, reinforcing the impression that the person is truthful and invested in the discussion. It may also facilitate rapport-building, which typically aligns with honest discourse. Conversely, nodding could be a tactic to placate the interlocutor or to feign understanding while formulating a deceptive narrative.
